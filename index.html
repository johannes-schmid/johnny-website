The Method

Personal
Recalibration
Method

A 4-phase process designed to help you release what's draining you, reconnect with your center and move forward with clarity.

01
Release & Clarity
Weeks 1–3. Get out of mental overload. Identify energy leaks, review what you're carrying, and find the relief of real order.
02
Re-Centering
Weeks 4–6. Return to yourself. Belief review, nervous system recalibration, breathwork, meditation and foundation rebuilding.
03
Relationships & Self-Worth
Weeks 7–9. Close the past without drama. Heal abandonment wounds, restructure self-worth, move forward lighter.
04
Life Direction
Weeks 10–12. Build your future with meaning. Why do you work? How do you want to live? A new self-agreement with renewed energy.
